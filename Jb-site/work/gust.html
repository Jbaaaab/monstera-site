02

General Direction

Art Direction

Gust isn't one agency, it's four offers living under the same roof, each one having its own visual territory (lol), and obviously its own prospects. The real design challenge was there : how do you build a unified website without flattening everything into one generic identity. The answer was already in the branding : gradients I made previously (the goat, thanks to me everything's solved once again). Each offer inherits a color, all pulled from the same tonal family. Coherence through system logic.

Des créateurs de contenus avec une communauté engagée, qui sont utilisés pour leur créativité, leur image et leur expérience sur les réseaux sociaux. Des égéries des temps modernes.

Des experts du social media qui orchestrent votre présence sur les plateformes. De la stratégie éditoriale à la création de contenus, ils transforment votre marque en média vivant, pensé pour engager et performer. Les storytellers du scroll quotidien.

Open, notre entité celebrity marketing. En association avec Isabelle Brulier, OPEN vous donne accès aux célébrités françaises et internationales pour vos campagnes, RSE, B2C et même B2B.

Les artistes du digital, aux spécialités diversifiées. De la 3D au stop-motion ou même au sound design, ils créent des contenus à la frontière entre le réel et l'imaginaire.

So, the objective was to create something that explains all of the offers through a limited amount of pages, while keeping a website that was clear, simple, beautiful, and as SEO friendly as possible. Luckily enough, we had plenty of time. It's false, the whole thing had to be done in three weeks, which is a plot twist worth mentioning IMO.

01

Hero Section

No headline trying to explain everything at once. An animated phone playing real campaign footage : the agency shows before it tells. As you scroll, the phone flips to reveal the core speech, the logos of past collaborators, and a CTA pointing toward the work methodology. Information arriving in the order it actually makes sense, not in the order it was easiest to write.

01

Studio Page

Hero Section

Website

The studio is the core of what Gust produces, so we made the hero feel like it. Dark full-bleed background, a single strong line — “Un studio pensé pour vos créations” — and one CTA to book it. No feature listing above the fold. You experience the space before you read the specs.

02

Studio Features

Website

Four accordion rows — 120m², Set up pro, Studio 360, Une régie OP. Each one expands into a full-bleed background image. Navigating the features feels like touring the actual space, which is a great excuse to show good photography without cramming everything above the fold. I love an excuse.

01

Case Studies Page

Website

We went full Gust blue. Not subtle blue — actual proper blue. The filtering system runs three ways in parallel : an interactive opening sentence you click through, badge tags on each case card, and a top filter bar. Three entry points for the same data, because not everyone explores the same way, and I don’t want to pick sides.
