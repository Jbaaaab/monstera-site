02

Direction générale

Art Direction

The brief was simple in theory : create something soft enough to feel warm, sharp enough to feel like design. Miffy and Kuromi aren't random picks — they carry decades of cultural weight in the cute space, and using them as visual anchors meant the language was already understood by the audience. The job was to channel that without being derivative. Pastel palettes earned their place. So did the photography — real textures, real light, nothing stock.

Visual identity system, social media art direction, event collateral, and on-site activations.
